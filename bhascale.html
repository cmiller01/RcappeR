bhascale

The bhascale dataset contains data for the lengths per second scale used by the BHA (source) when determining the margins beaten. This dataset is called by the bha_ls function when converting margins between horses from lengths back into seconds.

data(bhascale)

The dataset has 18 rows and 4 columns.

Variables

variable details
race_type type, or class, of race, (ie. “clm”, “mdn clm”, etc)
going official going
scale lengths per second scale
conditions abbreviated race conditions
